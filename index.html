Visit Montalban

Enjoy peace and nature in this tranquil province close to Metro Manila.

Top three activities to do at Montalban

Interact with animals

The Avilon Zoo is a great place for seeing many species of wildlife .

Take a hike on the mountains

Mt.Parawagan is one of the mountains that you and your friends can take a hike on.

Relax at a resort

Many waterpark resorts are present for a relaxed and peaceful time.
